Get to Know Me!

I'm 14 years old and I live in Brooklyn, New York. I was born in Rio de Jainero, Brazil , and most of my mom's family is still there. I moved to the USA when I was about 2 years old, and I visit Brazil often. My father is Greek, Polish, and Lithuanian, but growing up I was mainly exposed to Brazilian and Greek culture. I am a rising lower at Phillips Academy, and I will be living in Stevens next year. I lived in Isham last year, and I am rooming with Hilena and Donnally this summer in Paul Revere. My main hobbies include playing and listening to music, dancing, hanging out with my friends, and scrolling through Instagram.
